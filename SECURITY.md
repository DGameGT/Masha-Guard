# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in MashaGuard, do not open a public issue. Public disclosure before a fix is available puts all users at risk.

Submit your report privately through one of the following channels:

- **Email**: security@dgxo.my.id
- **Telegram**: @DGameXO

We will acknowledge your report within **48 hours** and provide a status update as the investigation progresses.

---

## What to Include

To help us triage your report efficiently, please provide:

- A clear description of the vulnerability and its potential impact
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Affected component or endpoint (if known)
- Any proof-of-concept code or screenshots (if applicable)

---

## Scope

Reports are considered in-scope if they affect:

- The MashaGuard Server and its API endpoints
- The `masha.js` SDK and forensic modules
- The HMAC handshake authentication mechanism
- The dashboard and session management

Out-of-scope: third-party dependencies, social engineering, physical access scenarios, and issues already publicly disclosed.

---

## Bug Bounty

MashaGuard does not currently operate a formal bug bounty program. However, valid reports with critical or high-severity impact may be eligible for recognition at the discretion of the development team.
